Nelson Mandela

The iconic South African Freedom Fighter

Timeline

  1. 1918: Born Rolihlahla Mandela in Transkei, South Africa.
  2. 1925: Attends primary school near Qunu
  3. 1930: Father dies. Entrusted to Thembu Regent Jongintaba Dalindyebo at the age of 12
  4. 1934: Initiated. Attends Clarkebury Boarding Institute, Engcobo
  5. 1937: Attends Healdtown, the Wesleyan College at Fort Beaufort
  6. 1939: Enrols at the University College of Fort Hare, Alice
  7. 1941: Escapes an arranged marriage; becomes a mine security officer; starts articles at the law firm Witkin, Sidelsky & Eidelman
  8. 1942: Completes BA through the University of South Africa
  9. 1943: Graduates with BA from Fort Hare; Enrols for an LLB at Wits University
  10. 1944: Co-founds the ANC Youth League (ANCYL); marries Evelyn Ntoko Mase
  11. 1948: Elected national secretary of the ANCYL
  12. Elected President of the ANCYL
  13. 1952: Defiance Campaign begins; Arrested and charged for violating the Suppression of Communism Act; Elected Transvaal ANC President; Convicted with J.S Moroka, Walter Sisulu and 17 others under the Suppression of Communism Act; Sentenced to nine months imprisonment with hard labour, suspended for two years; Elected first of ANC deputy presidents; Opens South Africa’s first black law firm with Oliver Tambo
  14. 1953: Devises the M-Plan for the ANC’s future underground operations
  16. 1955: Watches as the Congress of the People at Kliptown adopts the Freedom Charter
  18. 1956Arrested and later joins 155 others on trial for teason. All are acquitted by 29 March 1961
  20. 1958: Divorces Evelyn Mase; Marries Nomzamo Winnie Madikizela (1960)
  22. 1960: Sharpeville Massacre.A State of Emergency is imposed & Mandela is detained. The ANC is banned
  24. 1961: Goes underground; Umkhonto weSizwe (MK) is formed
  26. 1962: Leaves the country for military training. Sentenced to 5 years in prison for incitement
  28. 1963: Sent to Robben Island
  30. 1982: Mandela, Sisulu, Raymond Mhlaba & Andrew Mlangeni and later Ahmed Kathrada are sent to Pollsmoor Prison
  32. 1988: Moved to Victor Verster Prison in Paarl where he is held for 14 months in a cottage
  34. 1990: ANC is unbanned. Mandela is released. Elected ANC Deputy President
  36. 1993: Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize
  38. 1994: Elected by Parliament as first president of a democratic South Africa (SA). Inaugurated as President of SA
  40. 1996: Divorces Winnie Mandela
  42. 1998: Marries Graca Machel on his 80th birthday
  44. 1999: Steps down after one term as President; establishes the Nelson Mandela Foundation
  46. 2007: Attends the installation of his grandson Mandla as chief of the Mvezo Traditional Council
  48. 2011: Admitted to hospital in Johannesburg. Discharged after two nights
  50. 2013: Admitted to hospital.Passes away at home in Johannesburg
